Sunset in Marseille

Some infos about Marseille

Marseille (Marsiho in Provençal) is the main French city on the Mediterranean coast of Provence and capital of the Bouches-du-Rhône department.

Culture point on what a sunset is

Sunset is the time when the Sun disappears behind the horizon, in the westerly direction on Earth.

Why people love sunset ?

It's a friendly moment with a beautiful view where we can have an aperitif, tell each other about our day… In Marseille, the sunsets are legendary.
